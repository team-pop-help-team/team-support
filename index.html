Importance of Heart Health

28 october 2023

A healthy heart is synonymous with a robust cardiovascular system, reducing the risk of conditions like heart disease, stroke, and hypertension. Adopting heart-friendly habits, such as a balanced diet, regular exercise, and stress management, contributes to maintaining optimal blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

Beyond the physical, a healthy heart supports emotional well-being. Adequate blood flow to the brain ensures cognitive function, and the release of endorphins during exercise promotes a positive mood.

Post Covid-19 impact of health

2 November 2023

The post-COVID era presents a landscape where understanding and addressing the lingering impacts on health are crucial. Individuals who have recovered from COVID-19 may experience a range of physical and mental health challenges. One of the most common post-COVID symptoms is fatigue, which can persist for weeks or months.

Respiratory issues, such as shortness of breath and lingering cough, may also endure beyond the acute phase.

Harmful Steroids

23 November 2023

The term "harmful steroids" typically refers to anabolic steroids, a class of synthetic substances related to the male sex hormone testosterone. While these steroids have legitimate medical uses, such as treating hormonal imbalances and certain types of anemia, their misuse for non-medical purposes can lead to severe health risks.

Athletes and bodybuilders sometimes misuse anabolic steroids to enhance muscle growth and performance.
